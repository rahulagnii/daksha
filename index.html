About Daksha

Daksha

An Inter College Online Arts Fest providing an amazing platform to retouch the talents that are restrained by the Covid-19. A three day festival starting from 12th may to 14th may 2020, will be one of the most amazing online arts fest that sticks more deeply to nature. 'Daksha2020', will give you the strength to shock wave the walls and showcase true hidden talents to this vibrant community.

Date : 12th May - 14th May
